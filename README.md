# crowd-management
🕉️ Crowd Management
Crowd Management is a smart temple crowd management system that ensures a safe, convenient, and hassle-free experience for devotees while helping authorities efficiently manage crowds.

🚀 Features
🎟️ Online Ticket Booking: Book tickets with specific time slots to avoid overcrowding.
📱 QR Code Entry/Exit: Scan for seamless entry and exit.
🗺️ Interactive Temple Map: Shows gates, darshan areas, toilets, prasad counters, parking, and real-time crowd density (Green → Low, Yellow → Medium, Red → High).
🤖 AI Queue Management: Detects congestion and suggests alternate routes.
🚨 SOS / Emergency Alerts: Notify authorities instantly with your location.
🎥 Virtual / Live Darshan: Experience temple rituals from home.
⏰ Time-Limit Management: Ensures devotees exit after allotted time, maintaining safety and privacy.
